PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION

1.1 Introduction

Our selected problem is from Aquaculture Industry

Persona : Owner of Koi fishing business

1.2 Problem Statement

Ammonia and nitrite are just two of the many problems that result in hypoxia in the water that can cause fish kills (unexpected death of a number of fish).
The DPI (Department of Primary Industries) keeps a nationwide database of reported fish kills and discovered an average of 40 fish kills annually.

1.3 user needs

A solution that can prevent sudden death of the fish .
It will help the fish seller to maximize his profit .
The solution needs to be affordable, long lasting and easy to install .
The solution will also let fish seller to identify infected fish easily .
